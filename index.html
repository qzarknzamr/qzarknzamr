FUFA

Dibuat oleh: Quinn Aradea Ammar Kanza

FUFA stands for "Focus Until Finish Action," a motivational principle that encourages persistence and determination. It reminds us to stay focused on our objectives, keep pushing forward, and take action until we reach the finish line.
